## 3. The Extraction Algorithm
How do we calculate it?
1. **Ingest:** Take 100 hours of raw, unscripted audio/text from the subject.
2. **Atomize:** Break down into semantic clauses.
3. **Filter:** Remove standard English stopwords and domain-generic jargon.
4. **Identify Anomalies:** Find high-frequency idiosyncratic phrases (TF-IDF vs baseline).
5. **Prosody/Pathos Analysis:** Analyze the audio waveform (or text punctuation/density) to find where the subject speaks fastest, loudest, or with the most variation.
6. **Intersection:** Where idiosyncratic vocabulary meets high-pathos delivery, you find the Natural Center.
